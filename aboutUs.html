About Motivation Group

Welcome and congratulations to everyone on behalf of Motive Group. We are working globally, and we invite you to stay motivated with us. Motive is a social voluntary organization. Our journey began with the hope of standing beside the underprivileged and poor, starting with blood donation. We decided to work together as a group of young individuals to support society, find blood donors, and assist patients. Our voluntary organization, Motivation Group, was established on February 2, 2016, and our field-level work began on February 2, 2017.

Our Goals

VitalDrops Motivative is a social activity group. Our goal is to support the underprivileged and poor in society, starting with blood donation and working towards social development. If everyone comes together with unity, we can bring about a change in society. Motivation Group is a completely non-political, drug-free, and corruption-free social voluntary organization.

Our Social Group's Main Objectives:

  • Helping and supporting helpless people in times of need.
  • Assisting in blood donation.
  • Encouraging people to embrace free culture.
  • Uniting every individual.
  • Working with people through campaigns.
  • Increasing the number of blood donors.

Features:

We do not collect blood; we collect blood donors. When needed, our donors personally go to the hospital to donate blood. For any advice, opinions, or complaints regarding the group, you can inform the authorities. The authorities will evaluate your feedback.
